the cheese is made of moon

Sculpture and Code, 2014

A moon meteorite was purchased online, ground up, and added to a milk culture; which was then used to make a homestead-style hard cheese. This cheese was coated in wax and placed inside a temperature controlled environment within a suitcase that belonged to my grandfather.

This sculpture contains: Kingfisher Dairy Raw Cows Milk, Moon Meteorite 5406-recovered in Western Sahara, Africa, Rennet, Wax, Suitcase (Lee Horwitz), Wood, Glass, Arduino Micro-controller, Thermometer, OLED, Peltier, Fan, Wires
