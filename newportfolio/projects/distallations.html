Distillations Podcast Animations

Animation, 2017

Distillations is the in-house podcast and publication of the Science History Institute in Philadelphia, PA. I was commissioned to produce five animations based on popular podcast episodes.

Fizzy Water

The history and science behind adding bubbles in water and scientist Joseph Priestly.

Hippolyte Mège-Mouriès’s Recipe for Margarine

A short anecdote behind how to make artifical butter.

How to Mine an Asteroid

Ever wanted to know what asteroids are made of? This animation goes into detail and a little bit more.

Rethinking Ink

A short story about getting a tattoo.

Here Comes the Spray Man

An animation drawn from episode 207 of Distillations podcast, DDT: The Britney Spears of Chemicals.
