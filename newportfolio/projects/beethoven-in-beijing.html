Beethoven in Beijing

Animation, 2021

Beethoven in Beijing is a feature length documentary chronicling the historic journey of the Philadelphia Orchestra to China in 1973 from History Making Productions. I completed five minutes of hand-drawn digital animation, including storyboarding, character design, inking, animation, and editing. The film was broadcast nationwide on PBS on Great Performances in Spring 2021.
