These poems were generated by webscraping the forums of Contractor Talk and Skyscraper City. The resulting text was parsed by a natural language processing searching for combinations of grammar based on patterning found in William Wordsworth's I wandered lonely as a cloud. These remaining phrases were recombined together to form new poems and meditations on the idea of building within the natural world.


Created during SFPC Scrapism, Fall 2022
