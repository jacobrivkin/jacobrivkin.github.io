Jacob Rivkin


Jacob Rivkin explores how we internalize and experience landscape through memory, desire, and wonder. His work ranges from experimental animation, public installation, archival research, and incorporating real time data into sculpture. He is a lecturer of Art & Design courses at the Weitzman School of Design, University of Pennsylvania and Mason Gross School of the Arts at Rutgers University. He lives and works in Philadelphia.
